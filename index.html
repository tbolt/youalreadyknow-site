Band

You Already Know is a project that was born in 2011. Starting at local bars and quickly moving on to play popular Baltimore venues including 8x10, Ottobar, and The Metro Gallery. The group has since supported several national and regional acts and were featured on 98 Rock radio.

Each live show is filled with fresh originals and a wide range of covers from artists like the Grateful Dead, Daft Punk, the Allman Brothers, and many more.
